Job Seekers' Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Bdwomensjobs.com?

Bdwomensjobs.comLtd. is the largest and leading career management site in Bangladesh. It has started its venture since July 2000 with the aim to facilitate the job seekers to manage their career more efficiently. This site also helps employers to manage many of there tasks associated recruitment process more efficiently and allow them to save time and money.

2. How can I submit my resume?

The process of submitting your resume at Bdwomensjobs.comis relatively very easy. Simply click the Create New Account link or Post Resume option at home page. The Bdwomensjobs.comsite will guide you to submit your resume through five consecutive steps.

3. How can I edit my resume?

To edit your resume follow the steps:

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account using your User ID and Password.
  • After successful sign in, click Edit Resume option.
  • Now, choose any part of your resume to edit.
  • After updating information click on Save button.

4. Does it cost to post my resume?

No, it is absolutely free and you don't have to pay any cost to submit your resume at Bdwomensjobs.com.

5. What is User ID/Password?

The User ID/Password is your sign in credential to your Bdwomensjobs.com's account. With the User ID/Password, you can sign in to MY Bdwomensjobs account for managing your resume.

6. If I lost my User ID how can I retrieve that?

To retrieve your lost/forgotten User ID follow the steps:

  • Click on "Forgot User ID/Password?" below your sign in option.
  • A new window will appear; here you will get three options (User ID, Password and User ID & Password) to select. Select the User ID and submit it.
  • Now, type the e-mail address or mobile number which you have provided while creating your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • You will get an option that shows your name and User ID, select the option and submit it.
  • After that provide your date of birth to verify your account.
  • Your User ID has been sent to your e-mail address or mobile number.

7. If I lost my Password how can I retrieve that?

To retrieve your lost/forgotten Password follow the steps:

  • Click on "Forgot User ID/Password?" below your sign in option.
  • A new window will appear; here you will get three options (User ID, Password and User ID & Password) to select. Select the Password and submit it.
  • Now, you will get two options of getting code, email and SMS. Select one and provide email address or mobile number and submit it.
  • You will get a option that shows your name and User ID. If those are right select the option and submit it.
  • Now type the Code that has been sent to your email/mobile number and then provide a new password and confirm it. Your password wil be changed.

8. If I lost my User ID/Password how can I retrieve those?

To retrieve your lost/forgotten User ID/Password follow the steps:

  • Click on "Forgot User ID/Password?" below your sign in option.
  • A new window will appear; here you will get three options (User ID, Password and User ID & Password) to select. Select the User ID/Password and submit it.
  • Now, type the e-mail address or mobile number which you have provided while creating your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • You will get a option that shows your Name and User ID. If those are right select the option and submit it.
  • After that provide your date of birth to verify your account.
  • Your User ID and password's reset code have been sent to your e-mail address or mobile number.
  • Now type the Code that has been sent to your email/mobile number and then provide a new password and confirm it.

9. Can I change my User ID?

Yes, you can change your User ID. Follow the steps to change your User ID:

  • At first, sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Now go to the account settings and click on Set/Change User ID.
  • After that you will get two options (Email/Mobile), select one and follow some simple steps to change your User ID.

10. Can I change Password?

To change your Password, go through the steps:

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Go to the Account Settings option and click on Change Password.
  • Type your old password.
  • Type your new password.
  • Retype your new password and click Submit button.

11. Where can I view job circulars?

To view job circulars follow the steps:

  • You can view the available hot jobs in the home page. You can find your preferred job from "Browse category" available in home page.
  • Available job list will appear.
  • Click on Job title to view its details.

12. What is Keyword search?

It is a special job searching option with specific words. Go to the Keyword search section and type the desired word(s) in the box (e.g., 'oracle', 'financial analyst' etc.) and click on the Search button. You will be directed to a job list page with job circulars carrying that particular word.

13. How can I apply to a job?

To apply a job you must have your MY Bdwomensjobs. Follow the steps and apply to any job:

  • Click Apply Online button.
  • Now you will be shown a message that asks whether you want to give an interview or not. If you agree then click on "Yes, I will appear in interview" and sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Now the system will ask you to put your Expected Salary, type the figure and click on Apply button.

14. Can I cancel my application for a position?

Yes, but you can only cancel if application deadline isn't over for that particular position and the employer has not viewed your resume.

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Click on Apply online option and go to the applied job list.
  • Click on the cancel icon beside "Expected Salary" of your applied position.
  • Now you will be asked for your confirmation of canceling application, click on "Yes". Your application will be canceled.

15. Who will view my resume?

Employers registered to Bdwomensjobs.com can view your resume, if you haven't made any change to your resume privacy.

16. I do not want my current employer or some specific employers to have access to my resume. How can I do that?

Follow the steps to edit the privacy of your resume:

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Go to the account settings and click on Resume privacy.
  • Now, select employer's name from the list at left and click on Update button.
  • The selected employer will not be able to view your resume.

17. How will I get informed if an employer views my resume?

To get informed if an employer views my resume follow the steps:

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account
  • You will find an option of Companies viewed my Resume and a numeric figure next to it.
  • The numeric figure represents the number of employers viewed your resume.
  • Click on the number to get the list.

18. How can I keep track which job(s) I have applied so far?

To keep track which job(s) you have applied so far, follow the steps:

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account
  • You will find an option Number of Jobs applied in last six months with a numeric figure next to it.
  • Click on the numeric figure and you will have your last six months' applied job list.

19. How can I change expected salary already applied job(s)?

To change expected salary already applied job(s), follow the steps:

  • After signing in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account, go to the option Number of Jobs applied in last six months.
  • Click on the number next to it. Now, you will have the job list which you have applied so far.
  • To change the figure of expected salary click on the previously given figure.
  • You will get an option to change your expected salary.
  • You will not be able to do this if the job application date expires or the employer views your resume.

20. Can I send my resume through e-mail?

Yes, you can e-mail your resume from your MY Bdwomensjobs account.

  • Just sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Click Email Resume option.
  • Now you will get options to type your e-mail, employer's e-mail and a short message.
  • After you fill up all these, click on Send Your Resume button.
  • Your resume will be sent automatically to the employer's e-mail address.

21. Is it possible to delete my resume?

Yes, it's possible to delete your resume.

  • After signing in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account go to the account settings option.
  • Now click on Delete Resume then provide your User ID and Password and explain the reason of deleting resume.
  • After typing those click on the Delete button.
  • Your resume will be deleted from Bdwomensjobs.com.

22. What is e-mail notification?

E-mail Notification is a process to inform you about the services of Bdwomensjobs.com through e-mail.

23. Can I set/edit my email notification option?

Yes, you can set/edit my email notification option.

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account and go to the account settings.
  • Now click on Email Notification option.
  • List of services of Bdwomensjobs.comwill appear.
  • Tick on your preference and click Add/Update Preference button.

24. Can I delete my MY Bdwomensjobs account?

Yes, ofcourse you can.

  • At first, sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.
  • Then, go to the Account Settings option and click on Delete Account.
  • Now, follow some simple steps to delete your account.

25. What's the function of favorite search?

This is an amazing feature of searching own preferable jobs.

  • It allows to save and search the Categories you like to visit repeatedly every time in just one click.
  • Here you can save a maximum number of 10 categories and can look for jobs
  • It's a trouble-free search feature that doesn't require selecting any category, location, keywords etc. again and again

26. Can I save more than one favorite search?

Yes, of course you can. In the favorite search option you can save a maximum number of 10 job categories and search jobs from there.

27. Can I edit my favorite search?

Yes, you can edit your favorite search. To edit the favorite search follow the steps:

  • First sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account and go to the favorite search option.
  • Go inside the job detail by clicking on the job title. There you will get an edit option through which you can edit your favorite search.
  • Beside this you can save any search as your favorite search from the job list, clicking on the save as favorite option.
  • Now you can add any other favorite search if you want.

28. Can I delete my favorite search?

Yes, you can delete your favorite search. To delete any favorite search follow the steps:

  • First sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account and go to the favorite search option.
  • Beside your favorite search name there is a Delete button in the right side.
  • Click on the Delete button to remove the search from your favorite search list.
  • Now you can add any other favorite search if you want.

29. What is shortlisted job?

Shortlisted job is such a feature that allows to shortlist/save jobs to view later. It provides notification as the expire date for applying comes closer and keeps updated with statistical report of job activities.

30. Can I save any job to view later?

Yes, you can save any job using the shortlisted job option. Follow the steps to shortlist any job to view later.

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account and click on any job title.
  • After you get the job detail click on Shortlist this job option in the right panel.
  • The job will be saved and shortlisted.

31. Can I follow any company for job information?

Yes, you can follow any of the employer or company of your choice using the follow option in the job circular. To follow any employer click the follow button in job detail by signing in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account.

32. Can I unfollow any following employer?

Yes, you can unfollow any of your following employer. To unfollow any employer:

  • First, sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account and go to the following employer option.
  • Now click on the unfollow button beside employer's name in the following employer's list. Your followed employer will be unfollowed.

33. How long a job can be saved as shortlisted job?

The jobs remain saved as shortlisted job forever even after the job application date expires. It will remain shortlisted untill you delete the job from shortlisted job.

34. What is an employability test?

A computer adaptive test that has several amazing features.

  • The test provides with a standard question paper and detailed report that helps to strengthen several qualifications.
  • Has a soft skill module that helps to know about candidates hidden soft skills.
  • This is a standard test that secures the next step of recruitment process and increase the number of interview calls.

35. How can I book schedule for an Employability test?

To book schedule for Employability Test follow the steps:

  • Sign in to your MY Bdwomensjobs account and go to Employability Certification.
  • Now, click on Book Schedule and select a category from Category and Test Name to continue.
  • You will get a list of available time slot and place for the test. Select one as your convenience and click on book now.
  • Your schedule has been booked and if you want you can change the schedule clicking on Change button.

36. What is interview invitation?

Interview invitation is a remarkable feature add to MyBdwomensjobs, which works like a bridge between the employer and jobseeker. Using this employers can invite the applicants for interview and applicants can get information about the interview along with chance of communication with the employers using the system if needed.

37. Where can I get information about interview invitation?

You can get information of interview invitation from different options in both web and app version of MyBdwomensjobs. The options are -

  • The bell icon in MyBdwomensjobs home page
  • By clicking on Interview invitation in MyBdwomensjobs main menu and
  • In app version clicking on interview invitation inside applied jobs. Accept these, if you sign in at your MyBdwomensjobs using the app version, you will get a pop up if there is any new interview invitation.

38. What tasks can I do in interview invitation?

The tasks you can do in interview invitations are -

  • You can communicate with employers easily using the system.
  • If you want to attened the interview on the scheduled time then you can confirm it using "Yes" option.
  • If you don't want to attened the interview then you can reject it using "No" option.
  • Accept that if the employer provides the reschedule option then you can request to change the interview time and date using the "reschedule" option.

39. What's the benefit of interview invitation?

The benefits of interview invitation is -

  • It builds a strong communication between jobseeker and employer
  • Jobseeker can get instant information about interviews
  • Sometimes jobseeker can request for rescheduling interview, if the employer keeps interview reschedule option active.

40. Can the date of interview be changed?

No, the date can not be changed right away. However, if the employer provides the interview rescheduling option, then the jobseeker can request to change the interview schedule of course with a valid reason.

41. What is interview rescheduling request?

Interview rescheduling request is a special part of MyBdwomensjobs. If an employer provides the rescheduling option, then a jobseeker can request to change the interview time and date according to his/her convenience. In this case one has to give proper reason. The employer may accept or reject the request.

42. How to request for rescheduling interview?

You can request for rescheduling interview with proper reason, only if the employer provides the rescheduling option in the interview invitation. You can find the rescheduling option inside the invitation and can request to change the interview time and date with valid reason.

43. How to know about different notifications from employers?

Employers mainly use two options to send notification. The options are -

  • System message
  • Email

Accept that employers can send SMS if they want.

44. What is "Video Interview"?

Video Interview refers to where employers conduct interviews by setting some questions and for selected or all applicants and applicants will record video answers of those questions and submit those recorded videos to employers. Basically it's an interview where the interviewer isn't present when the applicant answers the questions. Applicants can record and submit videos easily through MyBdwomensjobs panel.

45. How can I understand that I need to submit a video interview for a job?

If employer select you for Video Interview Invitation, You will be notified through:

  • My Bdwomensjobs Homepage
  • Email Invitation
  • SMS Notification

46. How can I respond to a video interview?

You can proceed for a video interview from your My Bdwomensjobs panel. After proceeding, you will see the detailed guidelines and procedure. You can User guide (link) for details.

47. What do I need to record video?

You will need :

  • Internet connection
  • Laptop or Desktop with webcam and microphone
  • Mobile device

48. How can I record video?

If you get selected for a video interview, you can record video through our system after signing into your MyBdwomensjobs account. Press "Start recording" button after reading the guidelines carefully. Once recording is finished you can submit that to the employer. You can User guide (link) for details.

49. Can I pre-view the questions before recording the video?

Yes, you can view the questions before recording the video.

50. How many questions can be set by an employer and what will be the total time duration for those questions?

The employer can set up to 5 questions and the maximum time for these questions can be last upto 3 minutes (180 seconds).

51. How much time will I get to record video interview?

You have to record and submit video within 1 hour once you start recording. You must have to complete a recording for each question within the set time by the employer. Please note that, you will get a maximum of 3 minutes or 180 seconds to answer all the questions.

52. Should I have to record videos in the sequence of questions?

No, you can record videos according to your favorite question but you have to record and submit all the answers of the questions within the given time.

53. Will I be able to preview my recorded video before submitting?

Yes, you will be able to preview your recorded video.

54. What will happen if my internet connection is lost during recording video?

You can record from where you have left if your internet connection is back within the processing time. You donâ€™t need to record those questions again you recorded previously. Record only unanswered questions. Please note that you can not resume your recording if the internet connection is not back within 1 hour.

55. Can I record and submit videos if I already have submitted once?

No, you canâ€™t record and submit video once you have already submitted.

56. Can I pause during recording?

Yes, you can pause during the video recording.

57. What should be done if I complete answering one of the questions earlier than the allocated time?

If you complete answering one of the questions earlier than the allocated time then click on the "Done" button to proceed to the next question. Do not wait to finish the time if you complete answering earlier than the allocated time.

58. Can i upload videos of my answers individually?

No you can not. You have to record and upload videos of your answers through Bdwomensjobs system.
