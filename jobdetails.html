Category: Production/Operation

Manager - HR, Admin & Compliance

BS Asia Limited

View all jobs of this company

Vacancy

01

Job Responsibilities

  • Must be able to manage HR, Admin & Compliance issues of the Knit Garments factory.
  • Implement Compliance polices as per local law and different buyers code of conduct.
  • Should have sound knowledge on Labor Law, Factories Act, Fire Prevention and Fire Fighting Law and ILO convention Relevant on RMG/Textile sector in Bangladesh.
  • Must be able to prepare factory for facing different audit such as BSCI, SEDEX, Better Work, Accord, OEKO-Tex, GOTS, social / ethical / environmental / technical / and other certifications.
  • Be able to ensure Fire, Health & Safety issues are in order.
  • Capable to monitor training and awareness on regular basis.
  • Must be able to communicate with BTMA, BTTLMEA, BGMEA, Compliance Auditors, Fire & Civil Defense Department, Labor Court, Police Station, Local Influences and Labor Federation etc.
  • Perform any other task as assigned by the management from time to time.

Employment Status

Full-time

Workplace

  • Work at office

Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor degree in any discipline
  • Applicant must have MBA/ Bachelor or equivalent degree in relevant discipline with adequate knowledge in Compliance of Garments & Textile Sector.
  • Skills Required: Ability to Work Under Pressure

Experience Requirements

  • At most 7 year(s)
  • The applicants should have experience in the following area(s):
    Knit garments
  • The applicants should have experience in the following business area(s):
    Garments

Additional Requirements

  • Age 30 to 40 years
  • Only males are allowed to apply
  • Must not be over 40 years.
  • At least 7 year(s) in 100% export oriented Knit Garments factory.
  • The applicants should have experience in the following area(s):
  • HR, Admin and compliance, Compensation/ Benefits/ Attendance/ Leave Management.

Job Location

Narayanganj

Salary

  • Negotiable
  • As per company policy

Compensation & Other Benefits

  • Salary Review: Yearly
  • Festival Bonus: 2
